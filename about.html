About Twil Tex

Connecting the World to Pakistan’s Home, Hospitality & Healthcare Textiles Since 1991. A legacy of quality, trust, and expert managed sourcing.

Factory / Our Story

Established in 1991 in Faisalabad, Pakistan’s textile hub, Twil Tex began as a modest spinning unit. Over four decades, we have grown into a vertically integrated sourcing agency, serving international buyers with high-quality Home, Hospitality, and Healthcare textiles. Each product category is managed by dedicated industry experts, ensuring quality, compliance, and timely delivery worldwide.
